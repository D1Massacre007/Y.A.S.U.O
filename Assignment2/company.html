About FoodShare

FoodShare is a platform designed to connect people with delicious food options, making it easier to browse, explore, and manage food items through a simple interface.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a clean, user-friendly food listing system where users can discover items like pasta, biryani, pizza, and more in one place.

Our Vision

We aim to build a scalable food platform that can later support real-time ordering, payments, and user management.

Why Choose Us

  • Simple and clean UI
  • Organized food categories
  • Admin dashboard for management
  • Responsive layout structure
